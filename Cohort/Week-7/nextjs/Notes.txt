Why server side rendering?
Problems: 1-Waterfalling 
            In terms of rendering, "waterfalling" typically refers to the sequential execution of tasks or processes, often in a top-down manner.

            In the context of web development and rendering, waterfalling can refer to the process of rendering a web page where tasks are executed one after the other in a sequential manner, usually starting from the initial request for the web page and ending with the complete rendering of the page in the user's browser.
